## Research Report: The State of AI Agents

Produce a well-researched report covering the current landscape of AI agents, including:

1. **Architecture Overview**: How modern AI agents are structured (tool use, memory, planning)
2. **Key Trends**: What's new in AI agent development (2024-2025)
3. **Applications**: Real-world use cases and deployments
4. **Challenges**: Current limitations and open problems
5. **Future Outlook**: Where the field is heading

The report should be evidence-based, well-cited, and suitable for a technical audience.
