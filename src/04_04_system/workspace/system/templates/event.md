# Event Template

Target: `world/events/{date}-{slug}.md`

## Required frontmatter

```yaml
---
title: "{event title}"
date: {YYYY-MM-DD}
status: seed
tags: [{relevant tags}]
---
```

## Sections

### What happened

Brief description of the event.

### People

Who was involved — link to [[world/people/]] notes.

### Place

Where it happened — link to [[world/places/]] if applicable.

### Related projects

Links to relevant projects.

### Outcome / Next steps

What came out of it, what to do next.
